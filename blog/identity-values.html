One of the big ethical decisions I have made was the decision to eat a vegan diet.
It came through a deeper exploration of my values.
Three key ones that always come up to me are.

  • Kindness
  • Compassion
  • Empathy

My awareness of these three main points had me look at my everyday actions and really ask myself whether I was living according to them.
